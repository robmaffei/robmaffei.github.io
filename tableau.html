Tableau & Power BI

Easy-to-use interactive visualizations with maps, graphs and charts.

Here we have a generic dashboard displaying AirBnB listings in Prague in March 2023 (data from Inside AirBnB).

This fully interactive dashboard offers the following features:

  • Hovering over a district on the map reveals detailed information about that area;
  • Selecting a district on the map, or choosing an entry in the district list or accommodation type, dynamically filters all elements for focused exploration.

Feel free to try out the dashboard.

While this dashboard is not tailored to a specific use case, it demonstrates the impact of visualization compared to raw tabular data: In this example the map provides a visual narrative by highlighting the concentration of listings in the city center.

It's important to note that different cases require different visualizations: for instance, in conjuction with data regarding the accomodation type, a similar map could be employed when analyzing the influence of short-term rentals on the housing market. Conversely, a different analysis might require another kind of approach altogether.

After all, it's important to recognize that there's no one-size-fits-all solution for the diverse questions we encounter.
