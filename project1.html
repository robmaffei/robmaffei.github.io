Project 1 - Products distribution and sales

Tableau Interactive Dashboard with an Intuitive Map for Informed Advertising and Distribution Decisions.

Overview: An interactive dashboard in Tableau, featuring an intuitive map for visualizing the geographical distribution of sales channels and product sales.

Client: A firm engaged in product sales across multiple countries through multiple channels.

Significance: Developed a vital tool for management, providing an intuitive and informative map of distribution points and sales. This facilitates strategic decisions to enhance advertising efforts and optimize distribution processes.

Tools used: Excel (data manipulation), SQL (data manipulation), Tableau (visualization).

Methodology: First I conducted a thorough assessment of firm requirements and project scope. I addressed data complexity by cleaning and pre-processing sales and distribution data using Excel and SQL. The following step was to Integrate data and craft an interactive visualization in Tableau based on initial specifications. Final product was iteratively refined based on feedback from end users.

Impact: The interactive dashboard is a key resource, guiding decisions related to localized advertising and distribution adjustments, actively contributing to the company's increase in revenue.
