Project 2 - Market analysis

In-depth Excel report on Sales Performance and Market Opportunities.

Overview: An interactive report featuring dashboards that display market segmentation and product performance in comparison to competitors.

Client: A firm with diverse product lines operating across multiple markets.

Significance: Addressed management's need for a comprehensive market overview to asses product performance in relation to competitors and identify market opportunities.

Tools: Excel (data manipulation and visualization), aligning with the company's preference for BI tools.

Methodology: Constrained by project requirements I leveraged Excel's versatility to perform all the steps leading to the final product. Automated data acquisition and initial processing were covered by Power Query, then I employed lookup and pivot tables for subsequent cleaning, exploration, and preliminary analysis. Next I designed an intuitive, interactive report resembling dedicated BI tools and incorporated user feedback through iterative development.

Impact: The report is indispensable for evaluating product performance and identifying strategic market opportunities, directly contributing to the company's revenue growth.
